Sample Referral Message

If you liked your experience with the Day Worker Center, please recommend us!  Telling your experience in your own words makes the strongest referral, but if you’re having trouble getting started, here’s a sample you can use or modify to meet your needs.

Sample Text: I recently used the Day Worker Center of Mountain View for help around the home.  I was impressed by the quality of work and the inexpensive pricing.  It was easy to schedule too. Call (650) 903-4102 or simply drop into the Center at 113 Escuela Ave. in Mountain View to get workers on the spot from 7am-5pm Monday through Saturday.

The Day Worker Center is a 501C3 non-profit organization established in 1996 by leaders from local businesses, churches, and the community to provide job-matching services for homeowners and businesses. The DWC helps employers find reliable workers, while empowering workers through education, advocacy, and job-skills training. Workers usually perform yard work, house cleaning, child care, and moving, but also tasks such as construction, painting, plumbing, electrical work, cooking, and sewing.

Ways to personalize your message:  (Let your friends know what type of services our workers performed and how much it cost per hour, how you felt about the work, etc.)
