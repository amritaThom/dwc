About the Day Worker Center of Mountain View

The Day Worker Center is a non-profit organization that connects day workers and employers with dignity. The Center helps employers find reliable, skilled, and friendly workers, while empowering workers to improve their socio-economic condition through education, advocacy and job skills training. Workers are most often employed for yard work, house cleaning, child care, and moving, but also perform tasks such as construction, painting, plumbing, electrical work, cooking and sewing. Workers are available for 24/7 by previous arrangement or employers may drop in: 7 am - 5 pm Monday through Saturday.
