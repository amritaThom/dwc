Moving Services

The Day Worker Center of Mountain View offers you same-day, reliable, safe moving for any moving job, large or small, including boxing, loading, and unloading of goods and furniture. Employers provide the moving vehicle and driver.


"I have hired day workers from the Center for multiple moves, from house to house, and moving my office to a new building. I have been extremely happy with the diligence and skills of the workers I have hired. They stepped right up and knew what to do. Their work ethic has been tremendous."

Sharon P., Homeowner, Mountain View

 

About Our Workers

All workers fill out applications and go into our database. The Center calls every employer to request an evaluation and tracks each worker's performance. They are reliable workers, ready to serve.

About the DWCMV

We are a fourteen-year-old non-profit agency that matches employers with temporary workers in an environment that is safe and dignified for both. The DWCMV is the 2009 Non Profit Organization of the Year of the Mountain View Chamber of Commerce and recipient of the 2008 Mayor's Award of Mountain View, as well as many other honors, and a member of the Chamber of Commerce.
